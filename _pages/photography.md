I’m a published photographer. Some of the places my photos have shown up:

- A [photo I took at MIT](https://www.flickr.com/photos/cdevers/5702488800/) ended up in a montage in [Apple’s October 2018 keynote event](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bfHEnw6Rm-4), as part of a commercial showing people using Macs. [My photo appeared](https://youtu.be/bfHEnw6Rm-4?si=yJ7ZuosR14IewtVw&t=310) between JJ Abrams (Star Wars & Star Trek!) and Paul McCartney (the Beatles!).  <a data-flickr-embed="true" data-header="true" data-footer="true" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/cdevers/5702488800/in/" title="MIT+150: FAST (Festival of Art + Science + Technology): FAST LIGHT — Man studying alone on his Macbook at the library"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/3530/5702488800_dc4b9f9865_b.jpg" width="1024" height="678" alt="MIT+150: FAST (Festival of Art + Science + Technology): FAST LIGHT — Man studying alone on his Macbook at the library"/></a>

A sampling of some of my other published photography work:

- A [photo I took](https://www.flickr.com/photos/cdevers/54058589362/) of the aurora borealis over [Prospect Hill Monument](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prospect_Hill_Monument) in October 2024 was used on [the City of Somerville’s home page](https://www.flickr.com/photos/cdevers/54061683459/). <a data-flickr-embed="true" data-header="true" data-footer="true" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/cdevers/54058589362/" title="Aurora borealis over Prospect Hill Tower, Somerville MA"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/54058589362_708bf4e992_b.jpg" width="1024" height="768" alt="Aurora borealis over Prospect Hill Tower, Somerville MA"/></a>
- In 2025, I contributed photographs for the [Somerville Museum](https://www.somervillemuseum.org/)’s [History On The Line](https://www.somervillemuseum.org/historyontheline-map) project for the 250th anniversary of the events leading up to the American Revolution. I took many of the photos on [the project’s interactive map](https://felt.com/map/History-On-The-Line-The-Map-xgPL04L2TFuwLVE7fD1efA?loc=42.38776,-71.11062,14.51z). 
- I’ve done in-house product photography of server hardware while I was at EditShare. 
- A collection of my photos of Somerville were used at the inauguration for Mayor Jake Wilson in January, 2026.
- My photo of a Banksy graffiti piece in Cambridge, MA ran in the Boston Globe on May 13, 2010: <a data-flickr-embed="true" data-header="true" data-footer="true" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/cdevers/4602001717/" title="Banksy in Boston: Overview of the NO LOITRIN piece on Essex St in Central Square, Cambridge"><img src="https://live.staticflickr.com/4028/4602001717_869ce16552_c.jpg" width="800" height="600" alt="Banksy in Boston: Overview of the NO LOITRIN piece on Essex St in Central Square, Cambridge"/></a>
